MDM Senior Developer

The Master Data Management (MDM) Senior Developer will serve as the lead technical resource for the development and maintenance of NYC Opportunity’s IBM-based MDM platform. They will work as a senior member of our technology team to manage the ongoing upgrade and development of the platform, and collaborate with key stakeholders to drive the long term data strategy for enterprise data integration.


  Responsibilities

  • Manage the upgrade and ongoing maintenance of NYC Opportunity’s Master Data Management (MDM) platform.
  • Plan, design and develop the appropriate strategy and solution within the IBM MDM Party domains (both person and organization).
  • Develop and configure enhancements within the MDM implementation using MDM features such as API and Search services, Extensions, Additions and Business proxies,etc.
  • Coordinate the efforts on the technical architecture (infrastructure) design with consideration of capacity, performance, and high availability for the MDM platform.
  • Install and configure the MDM system components, developing/updating scripts, deploy custom code on the new platform, and build production and non-production technical environments.
  • Define and implement management processes for maintaining data governance policies and standards.
  • Utilize data governance framework, technology capabilities, processes/procedures and implementation plans to drive data standards across the enterprise.
  • Write clear technical design deliverables to document development work.
  • Ensure integrity and quality of product deliverables through ongoing monitoring, testing and metadata management of data sources, products and initiatives.
  • Other duties as assigned.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Hands-on experience migrating IBM MDM from older versions (8.x, 9.x) to MDM 11.x Advanced Enterprise Edition.

Proven track record that demonstrates expertise with MDM architecture, InfoSphere MDM installation, configuration and development.

Strong experience with ETL programming and data modeling.

Hands-on experience with enterprise data warehouse implementations, integrating multiple legacy and strategic source systems.

Client focused, committed to rapid execution, and strategic and flexible.

  Education and Experience

Master's degree in information systems, technology or equivalent preferred with 3-5 years of experience in data integration and analytics.

The exam is a skills and experience-based Q&A test scheduled for Oct 3 - Oct 23, 2018.
