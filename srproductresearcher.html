Senior Product Researcher

The Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity (NYC Opportunity) works to reduce poverty and broaden opportunity by advancing the use of data and evidence in program and policy design, service delivery, and budget decisions. We partner with the Mayor’s Office for Talent and Workforce Development to manage a growing portfolio of workforce products including Working NYC, a website with information about workforce programs / services and employment opportunities for job seekers. The next phase of work will focus on continuous user experience enhancements to Working NYC and the launch of a front door for employers.

We are looking for an experienced Senior Product Researcher who can help us better understand the challenges employers are facing, how they are currently solving this challenge, who else is solving this challenge and what might the City be able to do to ease these pain points.

This is a full-time, contract position for up to 6 months.


  Responsibilities

  • Conduct research with City officials, employers, CBOs, and partners to understand the needs of employers and how we might approach building a digital tool to meet their needs:
    • Plan and draft research: brief/scoping, protocols, scripts, scheduling, and other logistics documents
    • Conduct workshops, interviews and other information collection methods.
    • Landscape analysis to understand what resources are currently solving this problem and where are the gaps.
    • Synthesize and organize information for different stakeholder groups (briefs for senior level stakeholders, detailed for product team).
  • Produce key insights and recommendations for what we might prototype to address employer challenges.
  • Support engagements during the design and development process to ensure the product concepts, design, and experience are aligned with stakeholder and employer expectations and solve their challenges.
  • Plan and manage continued post-launch research for Working NYC, the job seeker front door so we can continue to iterate based on community feedback and stakeholder requests.
    • Workshops, intercept testing, interviews and other activities to get feedback from users.

  Skills We're Seeking

We understand that candidates may not have all of the skills we are seeking below. We encourage those interested to apply even if they do not meet all of them. We are looking for talented individuals to join our team.

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Experience working closely with communities who are engaging with public services and programs and/or has lived-experience engaging with public services and programs.

Experience in policy, government or non-profit, in particular with low-income and vulnerable youth and families is a plus.

1-2 years of professional experience in planning, conducting, and presenting research.

Excellent communication and presentation skills, both verbal and written.

Interest and/or experience in human centered design tools and tactics.

Can organize work for yourself and delegate tasks to others.

Familiarity or willingness to learn data analysis methods and tools like Google Analytics, Google Data Studio, and Google Tag Manager.
