Policy Analyst

Within NYC Opportunity, the Enterprise Data Solutions (EDS) team supports meaningful data integration that allows City agencies to increase efficiency and access to benefits and services. To do this work, we use proven strategies to responsibly bring together data. In addition, the EDS team develops technology and applications that allow for independent rapid on-demand matching capabilities that operate within strict legal frameworks.

For successful data integration some key components must be present. The methodology employed by the EDS team includes knowledge gathering, user research, and data governance. This helps us pull together data in an intelligent and cohesive manner, and allows us to better understand the data and the consumer.

The Policy Analyst will be part of the Data Governance and Policy Unit within the EDS team. The Policy Analyst will research and develop innovative approaches to link data in a meaningful way, help inform decision making by City-wide stakeholder groups, and provide effective data solutions for New York City’s complicated data needs.


  Responsibilities

  • Develop policy memos to advocate or justify access to data.
  • Engage program staff and caseworkers in using integrated data systems like Worker Connect to improve case decisions and service coordination.
  • Prepare and facilitate stakeholder interviews, usability testing, and workshops for users of EDS data products and services.
  • Synthesis research to generate insights that inform design and functionality decisions for EDS products and services.
  • Develop and maintain a repository of product and user success stories.
  • Survey agencies data needs for critical policy objectives;
  • Research data sources and requirements to develop compelling cases for data sharing.
  • Research government data sharing best practices.
  • Track developments in data technologies and trends.
  • Write document deliverables to be shared with legal counsel, program staff and executive leadership.
  • Perform other duties as assigned.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Strong analytical skills.

Ability to self-direct work.

Client focused, committed to rapid execution, and strategic and flexible.

Proficient in MS Office: PowerPoint and Excel.

Highly organized and detail‐oriented.

  Education and Experience

A master's degree from an accredited college in economics, information technology, data science, finance, sociology, political science, urban studies or a closely related field, and two years of satisfactory full-time professional experience in government or non-profit sector.

A baccalaureate degree from an accredited college and four years of professional experience in the areas described in "1" above.
