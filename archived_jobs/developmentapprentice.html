  Responsibilities

Based on your skills and interests, responsibilities will include some but not all of the below:

  • Help build a pattern library that can be used across the digital products.
  • Prototype and test new product functionalities and enhancements with the staff developers, product managers, and designers.
  • Help build automated front-end and back-end testing scripts to ensure application functionality.
  • Help manage and enhance the back-end rules engine that powers the ACCESS NYC benefit eligibility application.
  • Assist in migrating an enterprise application from on-premise to cloud environment.

  Education and Experience

Apprenticeships are open to undergraduate, graduate, developer bootcamp or other training program students and recent graduates.

Note that all applicants must be authorized to work the United States. We are unable to provide sponsorship to apprentices.

  Skills We're Seeking

Basic knowledge of HTML/CSS and JavaScript and/or server-side programming language like Java, C#, .NET, Ruby, Python, PHP.

Familiarity with Content Management Systems like WordPress, Drupal, etc.

Experience with relational databases and SQL queries.

Experience with Git, Github or other versioning systems.

Experience with development methodologies like Agile, SCRUM, Waterfall, etc.

Interest and/or experience working with open source community.

  How to Apply

Please fill out the application form. The form will prompt you to upload a resume and cover letter. Applications are due on August 6, 2018.

Successful candidates will be invited for an initial phone screen. Due to the volume of applications received we may not be able to respond to every application.
