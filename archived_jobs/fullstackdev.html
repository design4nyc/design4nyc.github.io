Full Stack Developer

As part of the Technology Development Team, the full stack developer will be responsible for maintaining and expanding our current digital products and creating new digital services. They should have strong software development skills to make tools that are human-centered and help make complex information easy to understand.

  Responsibilities

  • Building dynamic, responsive web applications across front-end, back-end and database layer.
  • Building responsive sites from mockups and wireframes.
  • Develop and implement web services and APIs.
  • Collaborate with designers, product managers and technology development team members on prototyping and user testing efforts.
  • Coordinate development efforts with internal and vendor teams.
  • Implement security and data protection protocols in accordance with citywide security policies.
  • Provide production management support input for the design of new application builds.
  • Investigate and follow-up with various stakeholders (e.g., DoITT, vendors) to resolve issues.

  Skills We're Seeking

Proficient knowledge of a server-side programming language like Java, C#, .NET, Ruby, Python, PHP.

Familiar with back-end frameworks like Node.js, Express.js.

Proficient knowledge of most common databases including Oracle, Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, MongoDB, CouchDB, etc.

Experience working with cloud computing integration such AWS, Azure or private cloud environments.

Familiar with front-end technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Experience working with Content Management Systems (CMS) like WordPress and Teamsite.

Experience using and developing APIs and data web services including tools such as SMS communication, and working with data from diverse sources including case management systems, informational content, and other City, State, and Federal data feeds.

Experience with an agile, iterative development process and version control such as Git.

Willingness to learn new technologies and share latest best practices with the team.

  Education and Experience

Bachelor's degree in technology or equivalent preferred with 3-5 years of experience in digital services and technology development.

If you're a recent graduate of a developer bootcamp, we recommend you check out our Engineering Fellow position.

  Civil Service

This position requires applicants to take the Computer Systems Manager civil service exam.

The exam is a skills and experience-based Q&A test.

To learn more about the civil service process, visit the NYC Department of Citywide Administrative Services
