Product Design Fellowship and Apprenticeship

The application for the Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity Fellowship and Apprenticeship are now open!

A Product Design Apprenticeship or Fellowship with our team is an opportunity to grow your technical expertise while working on projects that help improve the lives of millions of New Yorkers. We are currently seeking candidates with an exemplary visual portfolio, leadership skills, and a strong interest in digital product design and storytelling. You will work on the delivery, on-going success, and continuous improvement of digital products and City services.

This position is offered as either an Apprenticeship (3 months) or Fellowship (6 months) at the same rate of pay (see details below). The position will begin in early September, with the option for a time extension based on performance. We are offering this role with a flexible title to be accessible to applicants with different schedules and levels of experience, but please note that there is only one job opportunity at this time.

  Responsibilities

Based on your skills and interests, responsibilities will include:

  • Sit on the Service Design Studio team
  • Work with Design Leads on project strategy and deliverables
  • Contribute to the design of digital products
  • Contribute to design research and usability sessions
  • Assist in the preparation of presentation decks and templates for the Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity, Service Design Studio and individual products

  Education and Experience

Apprenticeships and Fellowships are open to undergraduate, graduate or other training program students and recent graduates with a background or interest in graphic design, especially in a product development setting.

These positions are also professional development opportunities. We work with you to grow your skills, build your network and connect you to future job opportunities.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity through human-centered design practices in government.

Interest in using plain language to help residents access and interact with government services.

Leadership potential and experience managing multiple tasks.

A strong portfolio in any/all of the following practices: user experience, human-centered design, interaction design, user research, data visualization, and service design.

Strong experience or interest in digital design, product development experience a plus.

Ability to develop new design concepts, graphics and layouts within an existing brand.

Technical and design fluency and organization to support product vision and development.

Strong skills in Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud and comfort level with remote work.

Can-do attitude and desire to learn.

  Compensation and work environment

Our office is working remotely for an undetermined amount of time, so this role will begin remotely. We use Slack, Google Suite and Figma for easy remote collaboration, and we work hard to keep the sense of teamwork despite the distance.

The role will start at $17/hour.

  How to Apply

Please fill out the application form. The form will prompt you to upload a resume. Applications are due on a rolling basis.

Successful candidates will be invited for an initial phone screen. Due to the volume of applications received we may not be able to respond to every application.
