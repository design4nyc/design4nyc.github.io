Designed by Community Fellowship

The application window for the Service Design Studio’s Fellowship opportunity is now open!

The Designed by Community fellowship creates an opportunity to amplify the power and living expertise of residents who use or have used government services by equipping them with tools, resources, and a platform to design better public programs and services.

The program provides fellows with a fundamental understanding of service design, government structures and levers for change, and rapid testing of ideas. Through workshops, hands-on learning, and professional mentorship fellows can expect to gain skills in facilitating community-led design processes to collaboratively design (co-design) public services that are inclusive and accessible with the people who use and deliver them.

  Outcomes

Designed by Community Fellows will walk away from this program with:

  • A hands-on approach for navigating complex poverty-centric problems.
  • Strategies to align stakeholders and develop solutions based on community needs.
  • A process to improve solutions through collaboration, feedback, and iteration.
  • Basic skills in communication design, storytelling, technology, and public sector innovation.
  • A capstone project to apply skills listed above in a real-world context.
  • A network of relationships with the fellowship cohort and the broader government community.

  Education and Experience

Applicants must have a Bachelor’s degree (or are actively in the process of attaining a Bachelor’s degree). Experience working either in a professional or volunteer capacity with communities is a plus.Applicants who have lived experience using public services are encouraged to apply.

Applicants must also be New York City residents and be authorized to work in the United States. We are unable to provide sponsorship to fellows.

  Qualities We're Seeking

Deeply committed to centering low-income people in the design of government services.

The ability to work with people who have diverse backgrounds and experiences.

Can allow space for misunderstandings and mistakes in the process of learning.

Strong desire to learn with a proactive “can do” attitude.

Adaptable and have a growth mindset.

Able to think critically about problems and enjoy developing solutions.
