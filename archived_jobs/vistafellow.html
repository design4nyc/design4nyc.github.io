AmeriCorps VISTA Member

The application window for the Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity VISTA Member is now open.

The Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity (NYC Opportunity) helps the City apply evidence and innovation to reduce poverty and increase equity. We advance research, data and design in program and policy development, service delivery, and budget decisions. Our work includes analyzing existing anti-poverty approaches, developing new interventions, facilitating the sharing of data across City agencies, and rigorously assessing the impact of key initiatives.

NYC Opportunity manages a discrete fund and works collaboratively with City agencies to design, test, and oversee new programs and digital products. NYC Opportunity also produces research and analysis of poverty and social conditions, including its influential annual Poverty Measure, which provides a more accurate and comprehensive picture of poverty in New York City than the federal rate. Part of the Mayor’s Office of Operations, NYC Opportunity is active in supporting the de Blasio administration’s priority to make equity a core governing principle across all agencies.

The AmericaCorps VISTA Member will work with our team for 1-year starting in November 13, 2018 through November 12, 2019.


  Responsibilities

  • Conduct literature reviews, analyze research and reports, interview city agencies and services provided to identify best practices and inform new program development.
  • Create and implement work plans and liaise with city agencies and service providers to support the design and implementation of new anti-poverty programs for vulnerable populations.
  • Support the design and implementation of evaluation strategies for execution by external research partners to assess the effectiveness and impact of NYC Opportunity and YMI programs.
  • Develop a plan for enhancing NYC Opportunity’s existing knowledge management systems and protocols, and for supporting NYC Opportunity program leads to implement new performance measurement strategies.
  • Develop a plan to create new capacity building workshops or materials for city agency staff around NYC Opportunities core capacities.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Strong analytical skills.

Ability to self-direct work.

Ability to present ideas cleraly and concisely in writing and orally.

Substantial experience with project management, policy analysis, and research.

Highly organized and detail‐oriented.

  Education and Experience

Undergraduate degree or higher. Prior office experience and public service experience strongly preferred.
