Systems Administrator

As part of the Technology Team, the Application Systems Administrator coordinates the details relating to the infrastructure to support our applications from initial design through testing and deployment. S/he works closely with team members and partners to ensure high application uptime.


  Responsibilities

  • Coordinate execution of break fixes in the production and nonproduction environments, including hands on work in J2EE WebLogic environments.
  • Engage with the development teams to provide production management support input for the design of new application builds.
  • Work with Project Managers to determine timelines and project tasks.
  • Ensure that deliverables from the internal and vendor development teams meet requirements, including ensuring code matches server software stacks and specifications.
  • Provide support during testing and deployment by monitoring servers and providing feedback.
  • Manage the planning, documentation, coordination and execution of operational readiness testing.
  • Communicate project statuses to senior staff, executive management and stakeholders including preparing senior level technical reports for executive management.
  • Manage special IT projects as assigned.


  Skills We're Seeking

Investigate and follow-up with various stakeholders (e.g., DoITT, vendors) to resolve issues.

Experience administering and maintaining Linux OS servers, iPlanet Web Servers, IBM FileNet P8, Oracle WebLogic Application and Portal Servers.

Ability to write scripts (Ant, Maven and Shell) for configuration management (CM), build and deployment activities, and routine day to day functions.

Understanding of virtualized (VMWare), distributed architecture with High Availability.

Single Sign On (SSO) configuration using SiteMinder with iPlanet/Oracle Weblogic Servers.

NetIQ e-Directory integration with Oracle WebLogic and FileNet Platform.

Familiarity with Subversion, Github, TeamSite, Wordpress, and building and deploying Portal applications.

Hands-on experience assisting developers with issues related to ClearCase CM tool use (check in/out, branching, and tagging).

Knowledge of Solaris Operating Systems. Experience with Sun Web Server and other J2EE application server such as Tomcat, JBOSS, WebSphere and application monitoring tool like Quest Foglight.


  Education and Experience

Bachelor's degree in information systems, technology or equivalent preferred with 3-5 of experience in digital services and technology innovation.
