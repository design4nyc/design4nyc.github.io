Web Developer (Short-Term)

Working with the Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity is an opportunity to grow your expertise in creating digital services and applications to help improve the lives of millions of New Yorkers. We are seeking individuals with an interest in improving our current digital services, prototyping new applications, and participating in the New York City civic technology community, using a human-centered, iterative, data-driven approach. A web developer will gain an understanding of working within the New York City Government and learn more about the initiatives of the Mayor’s Office for Economic Opportunity. Projects will focus on the delivery, on-going success, and continuous improvement of government services.

We are seeking a full-time web developer to work with our team for a minimum of 6-months with the option for a time extension based on performance.

  Responsibilities

The web developer works with Technology and Product team to build and maintain digital products. Responsibilities include:

  • Build dynamic, responsive web applications across front-end, back-end and database layer.
  • Contribute to our growing collection of open source repositories on Github within the City of New York Organization.
  • Collaborate with team members and take ownership of major feature development.
  • Collaborate with designers, product managers and other technology development team members on prototyping and user testing.
  • Help maintain existing digital products.
  • Participate planning meetings, technology discussions, and daily standups.
  • Create technology documentation including requirements, technical specs, and user manuals.

  Education and Experience

This opportunity is open to undergraduate, graduate, developer bootcamp or other training program students and recent graduates.

Note that all applicants must be authorized to work in the United States. We are unable to provide sponsorship.

  Skills We're Seeking

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity through best-in-class digital products and human-centered design practices in government.

Proficient knowledge of a server-side programming language like Java, Ruby, Python, PHP.

Familiar with back-end frameworks like Node.js, Express.js.

Experience with relational databases and SQL queries.

Knowledge of cloud computing is a plus.

Familiar with front-end technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Experience using and developing web services is a plus.

Experience with an agile, iterative development process and version control such as Git.

Willingness to learn new technologies and share latest best practices with the team.

  How to Apply

Please fill out the application form. The form will prompt you to upload a resume and portfolio.

Applications are due on a rolling basis and successful candidates will be invited for an initial phone screen.

Due to the volume of applications received we may not be able to respond to every application.
