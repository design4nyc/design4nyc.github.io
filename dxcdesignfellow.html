Design Fellow - Designed by Community

A Design Fellowship with our team is an opportunity to grow your technical expertise while working on projects that help improve the lives of millions of New Yorkers.

We are currently seeking fellowship candidates with a growing visual portfolio that includes demonstration of ethnographic communication methods which may include documentary filmmaking, photography, graphic design, and a strong interest in storytelling and strategic communications. Additional skills of interest include conducting interviews and creative content development (success stories, blog posts, website content). Projects will focus on storytelling the delivery, on-going success, and continuous improvement of design within a government context, specifically for our Designed By Community program.

Fellowships are temporary, paid positions that entail a minimum four day/week commitment starting November 2022 - April 2022.


  Responsibilities

Responsibilities include:

  • Lead the production of a Designed by Community visual communications strategy, including the UX/UI elements and content strategy.
  • Develop presentation deck templates and social media templates for the Service Design Studio and larger NYC Opportunity office.
  • Work with Designers on staff to create project deliverables.
  • Other work as determined within the scope of this role by the Design Director.

  Skills We're Seeking

We understand that candidates may not have all of the skills we are seeking below. We encourage those interested to apply even if they do not meet all of them. We are looking for talented individuals to join our team.

Commitment to the mission of advancing greater equality and opportunity.

Experience working closely with communities who are engaging with public services and programs and/or has lived-experience engaging with public services and programs.

Experience in policy, government or non-profit, in particular with low-income and vulnerable youth and families is a plus.

Desire to use storytelling to uplift and empower community-led initiatives. Demonstrates ethnographic communication and visual design skills with a growing portfolio.

Working knowledge of intermediate- to advanced-level equipment (DSLR camera, video, audio recorder, microphones)

Enjoys writing and carries the ability to develop narrative and communications to promote existing work.

Technical and design fluency and project organization to support product vision and development.

Ability to develop new design concepts, graphics and layouts within an existing brand.

Developing skills in Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma, Miro, and Microsoft Office and Google Suite to create new assets and build upon existing assets.

  Education and Experience

Fellowships are open to undergraduate, graduate, or other training program students and recent graduates with a background or interest in graphic design, product design, and/or strategic communications.

  Benefits and Compensation

The hourly rate for this position is $22/hr.

Note that all applicants must be authorized to work in the United States. We are unable to provide sponsorship to fellows.

  How to Apply

Please fill out the application form. The form will prompt you to upload a resume and cover letter.

Due to the volume of applications received we may not be able to respond to every application.
